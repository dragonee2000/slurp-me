About Slurp Me

We are a group of young chefs that want to bring real quality food to the masses. We use high quality ingredients that will never be used at this price range of food. We believe that what we serve is what we love, as the love will be translated into each and every bowl sent out.

We strive to improve day by day, bowl by bowl, and we hope that one day, our food will be something you crave for day in day out.
